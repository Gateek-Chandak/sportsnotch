Make your notch actually useful: live **2026 FIFA World Cup** scores right at the top of your screen, plus every game's key details, goals, cards, the running clock, and stats.

## Install

1. Download `Sportsnotch.zip` from the [latest release](../../releases/latest) and unzip it.
2. Drag **Sportsnotch.app** into your **Applications** folder.
3. The app isn't signed with a paid Apple Developer ID, so macOS quarantines it. Clear that once in **Terminal**:
   ```
   xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Sportsnotch.app
   ```
   Then double-click to launch. (Nothing's wrong with the app, this just removes the "downloaded from the internet" flag.)

It lives in your notch and menu bar (⚽), no Dock icon. Hover the notch to expand a match; click the menu-bar icon to hide or quit. Requires a Mac with a notch, macOS 14+.
